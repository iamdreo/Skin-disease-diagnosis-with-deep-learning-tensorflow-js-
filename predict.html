About us

Kynect health is an artificial intelligence company using deep learning for medical diagnosis, medical big data analysis and primary health care which will drastically reduce health care costs and make it more affordable all over the world.

Currently, millions of people die yearly & suddenly due to improper diagnosis, poor and high cost of healthcare, these are some of the factors that led me to create this company. My prototype software algorithm currently detects pyoderma and scabies at a 97% accuracy in less than 2 seconds with no internet connection once the app is downloaded on a mobile device or web browser in less than a second and in the coming months with funding will detect it at a 100% accuracy, diagnose more diseases including cancer and help solve many health problems in society on an easy to use software, we are taking Ai round the world for cheap .

There are believed to be 111 million children alone believed to have Pyoderma with many co infected with scabies and more. Rarely resulting in hospitalization or death, there is a high and largely unmet demand for effective management at the primary health care level, particularly for pyoderma & scabies(Andrews et al, 2009). According to the world bank, in the developing world 400million and 600million people are suffering from pyoderma and scabies respectively. Hundreds of millions more adults and children are suffering from more diseases, many diseases some don’t even know about because they haven’t been properly diagnosed including cancer and our software aims to detect all.

Our company also digitizes patient records, in emerging markets especially. These are still being done with papers and biros, using artificial intelligence(optical character recognition), hospitals will be able to transfer existing patient records to the cloud with their mobile devices simply by taking a picture of the document, we will perform big data analysis which we will then use to find trends and solve some of the greatest healthcare problems mankind faces. We aren’t just building an African company, we are building a global company that will tackle the problems of today and the problems of the future.
